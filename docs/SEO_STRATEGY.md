# LumaClean SEO strategy

Updated: 2026-07-16  
Primary market: Belgrade, Serbia  
Languages: Serbian (Latin), Russian, English

## Objective and baseline

The goal is to earn strong local visibility for transactional cleaning searches in Belgrade. No search engine or agency can guarantee a #1 organic position; rankings depend on relevance, competition, authority, user satisfaction and time.

At the start of this work, `site:lumacleanrs.com` returned no indexed pages. The domain is new, so the first priority is discovery and correct indexing. The initial site exposed only three homepage URLs and placed every service on the same page.

## Technical work implemented

- Fifteen static service landing pages: five services in three languages.
- Unique titles, descriptions, H1 headings, body copy and FAQs for every landing page.
- Self-referencing canonicals and reciprocal `hreflang` links, including `x-default`.
- XML sitemap expanded from 3 to 18 canonical URLs.
- Internal service directory on every homepage and related-service links on every landing page.
- `Organization`, `WebSite`, `Service`, `Offer`, `BreadcrumbList` and `FAQPage` JSON-LD.
- Search-engine verification hooks through `GOOGLE_SITE_VERIFICATION` and `YANDEX_SITE_VERIFICATION`.
- Open Graph and Twitter images and locale metadata.
- One primary H1 on the homepage.
- Critical hero images delivered through Next Image optimization.
- Full scroll-video download delayed until critical page content has loaded.
- Permanent redirect from the former public Vercel URL to the canonical domain.

## Required account actions

### Google Search Console

1. Create a Domain property for `lumacleanrs.com`.
2. Verify it with the DNS TXT record provided by Google. DNS is managed by Vercel.
3. Submit `https://lumacleanrs.com/sitemap.xml`.
4. Inspect and request indexing for `/sr`, `/ru`, `/en` and the five Serbian service pages first.
5. Review Pages, Core Web Vitals and Enhancements weekly during the first month.

### Yandex Webmaster

1. Add `https://lumacleanrs.com` as the primary site.
2. Verify ownership with DNS TXT or provide the meta verification value as `YANDEX_SITE_VERIFICATION`.
3. Submit `https://lumacleanrs.com/sitemap.xml`.
4. Set the site region to Belgrade and keep the on-site contact details consistent with the organization profile.
5. Request re-indexing of the homepages and Serbian/Russian service pages after verification.

## Local authority plan

1. Create and fully complete a Google Business Profile as a service-area business. Use the exact LumaClean name, one consistent Serbian phone number, accurate hours, Belgrade service area, real photos and the canonical website URL.
2. Add the business to reputable moderated Serbian and Belgrade directories. Keep name, phone and website identical everywhere.
3. Ask real customers for Google reviews after completed work. Do not buy reviews, gate unhappy customers or publish fabricated testimonials.
4. Build useful partnerships with property managers, relocation specialists, real-estate agents, Airbnb hosts and expat resources in Belgrade. Earn editorial links from pages where the recommendation genuinely helps visitors.
5. Publish real case studies only when original photos, neighborhood, scope and customer permission are available. A good case study should describe the starting condition, selected service, area, duration, price range and result.
6. Avoid bulk directory submissions, paid link networks and keyword-stuffed neighborhood doorway pages.

## Business evidence still needed

To improve trust and local entity confidence, provide these verified facts before publishing them:

- registered company or sole-trader name and PIB, if applicable;
- primary Serbian business phone number;
- real operating hours and days;
- public address, or confirmation that LumaClean is service-area only;
- year the business started;
- real team/process photographs;
- customer reviews and permission to quote them;
- clear cancellation, damage and privacy policies.

No facts should be invented for SEO.

## Measurement cadence

### First 30 days

- All 18 sitemap URLs discovered and indexed where eligible.
- No canonical, hreflang, structured-data or mobile-usability errors.
- First non-brand impressions for Serbian and Russian service queries.

### Days 30–90

- Compare impressions and click-through rate by language and service.
- Rewrite titles only where impressions are present but CTR is weak.
- Expand pages using real Search Console queries and real customer questions.
- Acquire the first high-quality local citations, reviews and partner links.

### Months 3–6

- Track top-10 and top-3 growth for the five service clusters.
- Build evidence-led case studies and district content only where real work was completed.
- Improve conversion rate per landing page, not traffic alone.

The commercial KPI is qualified cleaning enquiries from organic landing pages. Rankings are a diagnostic metric, not the final business outcome.
